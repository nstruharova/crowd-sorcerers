Game Rules
  • Select the correct meaning for ambiguous words.
  • You earn {{ Constants.initial_agreement }} points if your team reaches an agreement at first try
  • In case of a disagreement you can discuss between each other and try once more. Each player sees the options in a random order.
  • If the team agrees on your pick, you still earn {{ Constants.initial_agreement }} points. Every other player will earn {{ Constants.eventual_agreement }} points.
  • You earn {{ Constants.disagreement }} points in case of disagreement.
