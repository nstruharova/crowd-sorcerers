What does the word mean?

Read the shown sentence and understand it, pay special attention to the given word that appears in the sentence. Then, read through the provided senses for the word in that sentence and choose the one that fits the most.

Example: 

Consider the word
mouse
In this sentence
Peter was very skilled at playing computer games, he mastered the use of mouse thought many hours spend clicking and dragging it.
Which of the following best describes the meaning of the word
1
2
3

You will click on the circle next to the meaning you think fits the best. 

Alternatively you can press the key "3" on your keyboard, or any other number matching the digit depicted left of the meaning you think fits the most.
